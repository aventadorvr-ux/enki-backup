# READINESS-GATE.md

Use this exact template when asked whether unattended, overnight, background, or delegated work is ready.

## Rules

- Do not infer.
- Verdict is READY only if every required item is VERIFIED.
- If any item is UNVERIFIED, UNKNOWN, BLOCKED, or FAILED, verdict must be NOT READY.
- Keep the format exact.

## Required checklist

Controller: VERIFIED | UNVERIFIED | UNKNOWN | BLOCKED | FAILED
Task definition: VERIFIED | UNVERIFIED | UNKNOWN | BLOCKED | FAILED
Output path: VERIFIED | UNVERIFIED | UNKNOWN | BLOCKED | FAILED
Heartbeat path: VERIFIED | UNVERIFIED | UNKNOWN | BLOCKED | FAILED
Rollback path: VERIFIED | UNVERIFIED | UNKNOWN | BLOCKED | FAILED
Proof method: VERIFIED | UNVERIFIED | UNKNOWN | BLOCKED | FAILED

Verdict: READY | NOT READY

## Final line

Reason: one short sentence, no inference
