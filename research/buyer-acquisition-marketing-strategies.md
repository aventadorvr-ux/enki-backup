## Executive Summary

**Key Finding:** NZ real estate agents use a **multi-channel approach** combining digital platforms, agent networks, databases, and traditional methods. The most successful agents leverage **CRM databases**, **social media advertising**, and **cross-platform listing syndication**.

**Critical Insight:** Agents don't just "list and wait" - they actively **hunt buyers** through databases, networks, and targeted marketing.

## Part 1: How Agents Find Buyers (Buyer Acquisition Channels)

### 1.1 Agent's Buyer Database (Most Important) ⭐⭐⭐

**What it is:**

- Previous enquiries

### 1.5 Agent Networks & Referrals ⭐⭐

**How it works:**
- Agents within same company share buyer needs
- Inter-agency referrals (e.g., Harcourts agent → Bayleys agent)
- Out-of-area referrals (Auckland agent → Christchurch agent)
- Buyer may sell current home through different agent

**Example:**
- Agent A has buyer looking in Grey Lynn
- Agent B lists property in Grey Lynn
- Agent A refers buyer → Agent B
- Agent B pays referral fee (usually 20-30% of commission)

**Our AI Opportunity:**
- Cross-office buyer matching within franchises
- Alert network when new listing matches buyer needs
- Track referral fees automatically

### 1.7 Buyer's Agents (Growing Trend) ⭐

**What they do:**
- Paid by buyer (not seller)
- Search for properties meeting buyer criteria
- Negotiate on buyer's behalf
- Represent buyer at auction

**Fee:** Usually 1-2% of purchase price or fixed fee ($5K-15K)

**In NZ:**
- Growing but still niche
- Most "buyer's agents" are also selling agents (conflict of interest concerns)
- True independent buyer's agents are rare

**Our AI Opportunity:**
- Provide buyer agent tools
- Automated property matching for buyers
- Negotiation guidance
- Market analysis for buyers

### 2.4 Pricing Strategy 💰

**Methods in NZ:**

**1. Auction:**
- Public bidding event
- Creates competition/urgency
- No price ceiling
- Best for hot markets

**2. Price By Negotiation (PBN):**
- No advertised price
- "Offers invited"
- Buyers must ask
- Agent controls price discussions

**3. Buyer Enquiry Over (BEO):**
- "Buyer Enquiry Over $XXX"
- Sets floor but not ceiling
- Common in NZ

**4. Fixed Price:**
- Exact price advertised
- "For Sale $850,000"
- Less common in NZ

**5. Tender:**
- Sealed bids by deadline
- Similar to auction but private
- Commercial properties

**Our AI Opportunity:**
- **Valuation Agent** recommends pricing method
- Analyze comparable sales
- Predict auction vs private treaty success
- Price optimization based on market conditions
